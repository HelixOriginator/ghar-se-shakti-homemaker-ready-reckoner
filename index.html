Ready Reckoner – Key Sections

1. Digital & AI Awareness

  • AI is a tool, not a thinking human — it only follows instructions.
  • Voice and video can be faked. Always verify emergencies independently.
  • OTP is equal to your signature. Never share it with anyone.
  • Install apps only from official app stores and review permissions.

2. Legal Rights Every Homemaker Must Know

  • Domestic violence includes emotional and economic abuse, not only physical harm.
  • A woman has the right to live in the shared household, even if it’s not in her name.
  • Daughters have equal inheritance rights since 2005.
  • Streedhan belongs only to the woman. No one else can control it.

3. Fraud, Cheating & Social Manipulation

  • Guaranteed high returns almost always indicate fraud.
  • Pyramid schemes collapse mathematically — most participants lose money.
  • Government schemes never ask for registration or processing fees.
  • Emotional pressure, urgency, and secrecy are classic scam signals.

4. Sanatan Dharma – Truth & Context

  • Varna was based on qualities and work, not birth.
  • Untouchability is a historical distortion, not scriptural teaching.
  • Core scriptures emphasize equality of atman in all beings.
  • Faith becomes stronger when grounded in knowledge, not confusion.

5. Behaviour, Ethics & Social Conduct

  • Assertiveness protects dignity without aggression.
  • Boundaries are necessary for mental peace and family stability.
  • Saying “no” respectfully is a life skill, not selfishness.
  • Children learn values more from actions than lectures.

6. Government Resources That Actually Help

  • Ayushman Bharat provides free health cover up to ₹5 lakh.
  • Widow and old-age pensions can be applied through CSCs.
  • Mudra loans are collateral-free and women are priority beneficiaries.
  • Helplines and portals work — middlemen are not required.

7. Understanding National Development

  • Verify viral claims using official data, not forwards.
  • Progress is gradual — neither zero nor perfect.
  • Independent thinking protects against misinformation.
