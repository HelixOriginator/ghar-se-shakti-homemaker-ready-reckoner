Government Yojanas & Resources (Direct Access)

Ayushman Bharat – PM-JAY (Free Health Insurance)

  • Coverage: Up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.
  • Eligibility:
    • Families listed under SECC-2011
    • All senior citizens aged 70+ years
  • Website: https://pmjay.gov.in
  • Helpline: 14555

Free / Subsidized House – PM Awas Yojana (PMAY)

  • Eligibility:
    • Family must not own a pucca house
    • Priority to women-headed households
    • Based on income and SECC data
  • Website: https://pmayg.nic.in

Ration Card – National Food Security Act (NFSA)

  • Eligibility:
    • Low-income / priority households
    • No existing ration card in same family
    • State-specific income limits apply
  • Food grains at subsidized rates.
  • Portal: https://nfsa.gov.in

Women & Financial Support Schemes

  • Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana – collateral-free loans https://www.mudra.org.in
  • Widow & Old Age Pension – NSAP https://nsap.nic.in
  • Complaints Portal – CPGRAMS https://pgportal.gov.in

Important: No government scheme requires payment to apply. Use CSCs or official portals only.
